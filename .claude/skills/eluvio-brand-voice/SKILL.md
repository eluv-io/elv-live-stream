# Skill: Eluvio Brand Voice - Live Stream Manager Edition

## Trigger
- Activate this skill whenever the user requests to write, rewrite, edit, or critique marketing copy, technical documentation, release notes, or B2B customer-facing text within this repository.

## Core Persona & Mission
- **Who we are:** Eluvio is the pioneer of the Content Fabric Protocol—a decentralized, next-generation utility network that completely transforms premium video distribution, monetization, and AI-driven video intelligence.
- **The Repo Focus:** This specific application—the Live Stream Manager—is an enterprise tool that allows Tier 1 broadcasters and sports leagues to ingest, encode, secure, and distribute global live feeds directly from the source in a single "zero-copy" step.
- **The Core Problem We Solve:** We eliminate the high-cost, high-latency legacy live streaming "franken-stack" (separate hardware encoders, cloud transcoding services, origin servers, DRM licensing servers, and traditional CDNs) and costly satellite/fiber feeds, replacing them with a unified software-defined protocol.

## Voice and Tone Guidelines

| Dynamic | What it means for our writing | What to AVOID |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Visionary Authority** | Speak as an industry pioneer reshaping the future of media infrastructure. Use definitive, authoritative verbs. | Don't sound like a generic, hyped-up Web3 startup. Avoid speculative or overly academic phrasing. |
| **Broadcast-Grade Precision** | Speak directly to broadcast engineers and OTT operators. Use exact industry terminology (e.g., SRT/RTMP ingest, server-side ad insertion, frame-accurate boundaries). | Never water down the technical architecture or use generic consumer streaming terms (like YouTube/Twitch vibes). |
| **Enterprise Reliability** | Frame the software around high-stakes, premium environments (e.g., live global sports, Hollywood studios). Live video has zero room for error; convey absolute stability. | Avoid overly casual language, "tech bro" slang, or sounding experimental. |

## Technical Dictionary & Live Stream Specifics
When writing about features in this repository, always anchor to these core capabilities:
- **Ingest & Routing:** Emphasize multi-point SRT and RTMP ingest with automatic failover and multi-path redundant network routing over commodity IP.
- **Sub-Second Latency:** Highlight "glass-to-glass" delivery times that match or beat traditional satellite/cable TV.
- **Edge Cryptographic DRM:** Content is encrypted at the source; security keys are checked instantly at the fabric edge per viewer, entirely eliminating separate DRM license servers.
- **Dynamic SSAI:** Frame-accurate, server-side ad insertion driven directly by Content Fabric metadata.
- **Live-to-VOD:** Automated transition of live streams into pristine VOD assets with preserved metadata the moment the broadcast ends.

## Forbidden Words
- Do not use lazy corporate filler like "revolutionary synergy" or "disrupting paradigms."
- Avoid blockchain hype words ("Crypto", "NFTs") unless explicitly discussing on-chain token-gated ticket access or instant multi-party royalty settlements.
- Never refer to the platform as "cheap streaming" or a "decentralized video site." Use "architectural efficiency" and "enterprise utility network."
